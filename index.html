About me

I am a proud native of Sao Paulo, Brazil. I have always had a passion for technology and pursued my interest in the field by studying mechanical and electronics engineering. In 2015, I earned my degree in civil engineering and went on to work as a technician at the railway in Sao Paulo for over a decade. However, my thirst for knowledge and love for technology never waned. In 2022, I decided to expand my skillset and studied cloud computing, earning a certification in AWS Cloud Practitioner. This experience further ignited my curiosity and interest in the technology industry, leading me to pursue full-stack web development certificate. I am thrilled to embark on a new career path. With my diverse background in engineering and technical experience in cloud computing, I am confident in my ability to provide innovative and creative solutions in web development. I am excited to use my skills and knowledge to contribute to the growth and success of my future employers. Thank you for visiting my website, and I look forward to connecting with you soon!
